Edenrock is a locally owned and run business, based in the South of Edinburgh.


With many years experience, we are able to create beautiful and bespoke gardens.


We love to tackle anything, from small projects to large scale landscaping and garden resdesign.


We are skilled in:

  • sculpting natural flower beds
  • laying driveways, patios, turf and artificial grass
  • building walls, decking, fencing and functional outbuildings
